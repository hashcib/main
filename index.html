ABOUT US

Hash is the global crypto investment firm offering financial solutions in the cryptocurrency and digital asset markets. We specialize in providing digital financial asset-related services in the EMEA region and worldwide. In the running of our business, we leverage our vast network of international partners.

Our strategy is based on:

  • Professional knowledge of blockchain and related technology;
  • Extensive experience in investment banking;
  • Experience in crafting financial solutions in the digital asset market.

Our technology partner is QIWI Blockchain Technologies: a company at the forefront of the Russian blockchain market. This gives us a strategic advantage in selecting and analyzing projects.
